Dino DNA- Closer Than You Think

Modern birds are dinosaurs. Chickens and pigeons are more closely related to T. rex than T. rex was to a Stegosaurus. So every time you eat fried chicken… well, you’re basically dining on Jurassic royalty.

The Velociraptor Conspiracy

In reality, Velociraptors were about the size of a turkey… but Spielberg sized them up because turkey-sized villains weren’t very scary. Scientists later discovered Utahraptor, a raptor the size of a human — almost like the movie predicted it into existence.

Built-in Air Conditioning

Those crests and sails weren’t just for show — some species used them to regulate body heat, like living radiators. Nature’s version of a cooling system.

Many were colorful

Fossil evidence shows some had patterned feathers or scales, possibly for camouflage or display.
